Welcome to Cat Battle: RPG!

To play:

  1. Select your player
  2. Select the cat you want to battle first (but pay attention to your enemies HP)
  3. ATTACK YOUR ENEMY
  4. Each time you attack, your enemy's Health will decrease by your HP and your HP will double
  5. For every attack you make, your enemy will attck you back, decreasing your Health by their HP.
  6. Defeat all of you enemies to win
  7. But if your Health decreases to zero before you do, you lose!

Good Luck
